Who am I?

Hi I'm Oliver Yi Wang (王祎), a PhD candidate in Informatics (Track in Software), School of Information and Computer Science, University of California, Irvine. I'm a member of the Collaboration Research in Action Design & Learning (CRADL) Research Group, Department of Informatics(INFMATX). My Research interests broadly include: web mining, machine learning, social computing, social network analysis, social application, complex networks/modeling, and human computer interaction. As a Ph.D., I'm closely working with Prof. David Redmiles, and Prof. Brian Skyrms on various projects related with Collaboration, Global software enginnering, Evolutionary Game Theory, Social Network Analysis. Details on projects can be found in Projects section.

I am also working with Dr. Evelyn Duesterwald (IBM Research) and Dr. Patrick Wagstrom (IBM Research) on studying developers' behavior of using Cloud-based IDE. I also work with Dr. Simon Sun (Xi'an Jiao Tong Univ.) on using Dictator Game to infer the people's preference to Justice.
